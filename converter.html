🎵 How to Use
// MIDI → Sheet Music in 4 steps
  • 1
    Grab your MIDI file
    You need a .mid or .midi file from any source — game rips, DAW exports, or online archives.
  • 2
    Drop it in the upload zone
    Click the upload box or drag-and-drop your file directly. Your file never leaves your browser — 100% local processing.
    Drag & drop your .mid file here
  • 3
    Hit Convert
    The converter parses MIDI events, calculates tempo, and maps every note to Virtual Piano notation with precise timing symbols.
  • 4
    Use your sheet music
    Read the recommended BPM, then use the notation in Virtual Piano. Here's the timing guide:
    cdeTriplet — no gap (fastest)
    c eSingle space = eighth note delay
    c-eDash = quarter note delay
    c---eMultiple dashes = long hold
